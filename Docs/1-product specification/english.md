# Car Rental Management System (MVP)

## Overview

A simple Arabic-first management system designed for small car rental businesses in Lebanon that own approximately **10–50 vehicles**. The goal is to replace paper notebooks and WhatsApp conversations with an organized, easy-to-use system that helps owners manage their daily operations.

The system is **not** a marketplace where customers book cars online. It is an internal business tool used by the rental company to organize vehicles, customers, rentals, maintenance, and daily tasks.

---

## The Problem

Many small rental businesses still manage their operations manually by writing customer information in notebooks or exchanging details through WhatsApp. As the number of cars and customers grows, this leads to common problems:

- Forgetting when a rental ends.
- Losing customer information.
- Not knowing which cars are available.
- Missing maintenance schedules.
- Difficulty calculating monthly revenue and profit.
- No organized history of rentals or vehicle performance.

---

## The Solution

The system provides a single place where business owners can manage everything related to their rental operations.

They can:

- View all vehicles and their current status.
- Register customers.
- Create and manage rentals.
- Track maintenance and recurring service reminders.
- Receive reminders for upcoming rental returns and scheduled maintenance.
- View simple business analytics and revenue reports.

The system is designed to be simple enough that someone who has never used business software can learn it quickly.

---

## Core Modules

### الرئيسية (Home)

A simple overview showing:

- Available cars
- Currently rented cars
- Cars under maintenance
- Today's important tasks
- Upcoming rental returns
- Upcoming maintenance
- Monthly revenue summary

---

### السيارات (Cars)

Store information for every vehicle, including:

- Photos
- Brand and model
- Plate number
- Year
- Mileage
- Status (Available, Rented, Maintenance)
- Rental history
- Maintenance history

---

### العملاء (Customers)

Maintain customer records including:

- Name
- Phone number
- Location
- Current rentals
- Rental history
- Remaining balance (if any)

---

### الإيجارات (Rentals)

Manage the complete rental process:

- Select a customer
- Select an available car
- Rental period
- Daily price
- Total amount
- Paid amount
- Remaining amount
- Rental status

---

### الصيانة (Maintenance)

Track recurring vehicle maintenance such as:

- Oil changes
- Water checks
- Mechanical inspections
- Tire replacements
- Insurance renewals
- Vehicle registration renewals

Each maintenance item can have its own reminder schedule.

---

## Daily Workflow

A typical business day looks like this:

1. Open the Home page.
2. Review today's tasks and reminders.
3. Check which cars are available.
4. Rent a car to a customer.
5. Update payments if needed.
6. Return vehicles when rentals end.
7. Record maintenance whenever it is performed.
8. Review monthly revenue when needed.

---

## Design Philosophy

The system should feel easier than using a notebook.

Key principles:

- Arabic-first interface.
- Simple and clean design.
- Minimal clicks for common tasks.
- Mobile-friendly.
- Familiar business terminology.
- No unnecessary complexity.
- Built specifically for small Lebanese rental businesses.

---

## Long-Term Vision

The MVP focuses only on helping business owners organize their daily operations.

Future versions may introduce features such as:

- Multiple languages.
- Multiple branches.
- Employee accounts and permissions.
- Digital contracts.
- Online customer reservations.
- Payment integrations.
- Advanced financial reporting.

The first version, however, should remain focused on simplicity, reliability, and solving the everyday problems of small rental businesses.
